About DS TORKS VENTURES

Your trusted partner in quality animal feed since 2019

Our Story

DS TORKS VENTURES was founded with a simple mission: to provide Ghanaian farmers with access to high-quality, affordable animal feed that helps their livestock thrive.

Starting from a small warehouse in Accra, we've grown to serve over 500 farmers across Ghana, from the coastal regions to the northern territories.

Our team consists of animal nutritionists and agriculture experts who understand the unique challenges facing Ghanaian farmers. We work closely with our customers to ensure they get the right products for their specific needs.

Our Values

Quality First

We source only the finest ingredients and maintain strict quality control standards for optimal feed conversion.

Customer Partnership

We see our farmers as partners, offering educational advice and custom solutions for their farm's growth.

Sustainability

We respect local farming ecosystems and source sustainably to support the agricultural community in Ghana.
